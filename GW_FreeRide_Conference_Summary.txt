OVERVIEW
===============================================================================

This 5-day workshop brings together gravitational wave researchers and AI/ML
experts to explore how machine learning can advance gravitational wave science.
The conference features invited talks, discussions, flash talks, and 
collaborative working sessions.

- Morning sessions: 9:00am - 11:30am (invited talks/work sessions)
- Extended lunch break: 11:30am - 4:00pm (includes snow activities!)
- Afternoon sessions: 4:00pm - 6:30pm (discussions/flash talks)
- Evening: Social events
